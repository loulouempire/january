Based in Tema, Amon West is on the rise, on the move, and heating up with his soulful, sultry sound.

Hailing from Broward County, Florida, Amon has a rich familial background in music that reaches back to the Caribbean birthplace of Reggae. It’s not surprising that Amon is carrying on the songful legacy of his grandmother, Sonia Pottinger, who was the first female Jamaican record producer, producing artists from the mid ’60s to the mid ‘80s.

His talent moved him to release an emotion-filled EP, The Violet Room, which features Miami rapper, Bizzy Crook. Furthermore, she’s currently working with Jonas Jeberg, BEAM, Chris Brown, London on Da Track, Hit Boy, Rihanna and Timbaland. Current cuts include Saweetie’s newest single “My Type” which was just RIAA certified Gold, and “Dipped in Ice” produced by Hit-Boy.
